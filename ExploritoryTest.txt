Test Scenarios
1. Solid Particle Falling Behavior
Objective: Verify that movable solids fall through empty space and liquids, but stop at immovable solids.

Steps:

Select Sand (press 1)
Spawn a column of sand particles near the top of the grid
Observe: Sand should fall downward due to gravity
Spawn water below the falling sand
Observe: Sand should fall through the water and accumulate at the bottom
Spawn stone at the bottom
Observe: Sand should pile up on top of the stone and stop falling
Expected Result: Sand falls through empty space and water, but stops when hitting stone.

2. Liquid Dispersion and Falling
Objective: Verify liquids fall and spread horizontally.

Steps:

Select Water (press 2)
Spawn water particles in a compact group near the top
Observe: Water should fall downward and spread horizontally as it reaches the bottom
Create a barrier with stone particles
Spawn water above the barrier
Observe: Water should pool behind the barrier and spread horizontally
Expected Result: Water falls vertically and disperses horizontally when it can't fall further.

3. Gas Rising Behavior
Objective: Verify gases rise upward and disperse.

Steps:

Select Smoke (press 3)
Spawn smoke particles near the bottom of the grid
Observe: Smoke should rise upward
Create a barrier with stone particles in the middle
Spawn smoke below the barrier
Observe: Smoke should accumulate below the barrier and spread horizontally
Expected Result: Smoke rises through empty space and disperses horizontally when blocked.

4. Solid-Liquid Interaction
Objective: Verify solids fall through liquids.

Steps:

Select Water (press 2) and create a pool of water
Select Sand (press 1) and spawn sand above the water
Observe: Sand should fall through the water and settle at the bottom
Expected Result: Movable solids displace liquids and fall to the bottom.

5. Fire-Water Interaction
Objective: Verify fire extinguishes when touching water.

Steps:

Select Fire (press 5) and spawn fire particles
Observe: Fire should produce smoke above it and have a limited lifespan
Select Water (press 2) and spawn water near the fire
Observe: Fire particles should disappear when they come into contact with water
Expected Result: Fire is extinguished by water contact.

6. Fire-Smoke Production
Objective: Verify fire produces smoke over time.

Steps:

Select Fire (press 5)
Spawn several fire particles in an open area
Wait 1-2 seconds
Observe: Smoke particles should appear above the fire particles
Expected Result: Fire randomly produces smoke particles above it.

7. Immovable Solid Blocking
Objective: Verify stone blocks all particle movement.

Steps:

Select Stone (press 4) and create a wall
Select Sand (press 1) and spawn sand above the wall
Observe: Sand should pile up against the stone wall
Select Water (press 2) and spawn water above the wall
Observe: Water should pool against the stone wall
Select Smoke (press 3) and spawn smoke below the wall
Observe: Smoke should accumulate below the stone wall
Expected Result: Stone blocks movement of all other particle types.

8. Particle Stacking and Gravity
Objective: Verify proper stacking behavior under gravity.

Steps:

Select Sand (press 1)
Create a pyramid shape by spawning sand in layers
Observe: The pyramid should remain stable
Spawn more sand on top
Observe: Additional sand should slide down the slopes and stabilize
Expected Result: Particles stack properly and unstable configurations collapse.

9. Mixed Particle Interactions
Objective: Verify complex interactions between multiple particle types.

Steps:

Create a stone container (walls and floor)
Fill the bottom with water
Add sand on top of the water
Add fire to the sand
Observe the sequence:
Sand falls through water
Fire burns and produces smoke
Smoke rises and disperses
Fire eventually extinguishes or burns out
Expected Result: All particle types interact correctly in complex scenarios.

10. Boundary Conditions
Objective: Verify behavior at grid edges.

Steps:

Spawn particles at the very top of the grid
Observe: They should fall normally
Spawn particles at the bottom
Observe: Solids and liquids should stay put, gases should rise if possible
Spawn particles at the left/right edges
Observe: Horizontal dispersion should be limited by boundaries
Expected Result: Particles respect grid boundaries appropriately.

Common Issues to Watch For
Particles passing through solids they shouldn't
Particles not falling when they should
Incorrect horizontal dispersion
Fire not producing smoke or extinguishing
Unstable particle configurations not collapsing
Particles disappearing unexpectedly
Performance issues with large numbers of particles
